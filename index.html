Training Course Summary

The training course on Social Insurance Law No. 148 of 2019 aims to educate Human Resources employees about updates to the new law. Through this course, participants will be trained and learn about:

  • How to calculate insurance shares

  • Pension systems and increases

  • Insurance procedures and steps

  • Injury compensation and unemployment benefits

  • How to schedule payments due from companies, and procedures for following up on installments due from stakeholders

  • Understanding new insurance forms 1,2,6 and training on their use

  • Copy of modern insurance application forms
